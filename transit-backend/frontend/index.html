Terms of Use & Privacy Notice

Please read and agree to the following before using Commute Assistant.

1. Location Data

Commute Assistant requires access to your device's GPS location to provide nearby stop information, route suggestions, and real-time transit services. Your coordinates are transmitted to our servers only during an active chat session and are not permanently stored. Location access is mandatory — the service cannot function without it.

2. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address via Google's OAuth 2.0 service solely to create and identify your account. Your Google credentials are never stored by us. By using Google Sign-In you also agree to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. AI-Powered Responses (OpenAI)

Your transit queries are processed by OpenAI's language models (e.g. GPT-4). When you send a message, it is transmitted to OpenAI's servers along with limited conversational context. OpenAI may use anonymised data to improve their models, subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. By using this service you consent to your queries being processed by OpenAI.

4. Durham Region Transit (DRT) Open Data

Transit schedule, route, and stop data is sourced from Durham Region Transit's publicly available GTFS open data feeds, published under the Durham Region Open Data Licence.

5. Accuracy Disclaimer

Important: Information provided by Commute Assistant — including schedules, delays, route suggestions, and AI-generated responses — may be inaccurate, out-of-date, or incomplete. Real-time transit data depends on live feeds that may be unavailable or delayed. AI responses are generated automatically and may contain errors. Always verify transit information directly with Durham Region Transit before making travel decisions. Commute Assistant assumes no liability for inaccurate results.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your chat history and saved route preferences are stored locally on your device. Your account email and a hashed password are stored securely on our servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
